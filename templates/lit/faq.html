What is this site?

The main functionality of Literature Breakdown is to provide a hub for book lovers in which they can leave analyses on any piece of literature ever written.

How do I write an analysis?

Simply sign up and you'll be able to write your own analyses immediately.

How do I comment on an article?

Scroll to the bottom of the article and there will be a text box to enter a comment.

Can I choose to favourite an article?

Yes!, just simply click the "Add to favourites" button on the article
